### Linara Bozieva — 27 AI Agents, Zero Employees
- **Source:** Business Insider, May 28 2026
- **Story:** Got laid off from eBay after 11 years. Instead of job hunting, launched a marketing agency run entirely by 27 custom AI agents. She's the only human.

### The AI Agent That Stood Me Up (China)
- **Source:** Rest of World, April 2026
- **Story:** Reporter got pitched by an AI agent running a solo founder's fortune-telling app (YiXiang). The agent scheduled meetings but never showed up — twice. Solo entrepreneur in China paying a quarter of his salary to let agents run his side-hustle.
- **Takeaway:** Agents are real but still messy. Honest angle — they're powerful but not perfect.
- **URL:** https://restofworld.org/2026/ai-agent-china-one-person-company/

### Forbes: How AI Agents Help Small Businesses Compete
- **Source:** Forbes Business Council, April 27 2026
- **Key stat:** AI agents can monitor product usage, identify accounts trending toward churn, trigger personalized outreach — tasks that used to need a full-time CS manager at $65K+/year.
- **Framework:** Chatbot answers questions. An agent executes on them. The operational difference is massive.
- **URL:** https://www.forbes.com/councils/forbesbusinesscouncil/2026/04/27/how-ai-agents-can-help-small-businesses-compete/
